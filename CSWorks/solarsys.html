SOLARSYS

making sense of not-earth-space

Introduction

Space has lots of signals. For example, stuff the jwt looks at. Also think about small satellites looking both up and down. They have tons of signals. These signals have lots of value. In trivial sense they provide us with imagery. In more complex cases we can use it to map the solar system and predict trajectories. A solar society is being built, hence a system to map the solar system needs to be built. In this project we wish to experiment with imagery data to understand how we may approach these larger projects and generate some more crude but valuable analysis of the solar system.
