Original paintings

I like to paint. Its a way of expressing memories and visiting the past.
We know information decays. Each recollection is different and sometimes
the simple details can get lost. Paintings provide a way to encode, with
color, composition, style, etc. I like to put my own life's stories into
my paintings. I also like to connect back to antiquity and past artists.


Nonliving artists I take inspiration from:
