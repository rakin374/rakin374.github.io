Hi, my name is Rakin Munim and I’m a futurist and (amateur) computer scientist. I was born in Dhaka and moved to the United States when I was around 6 years old. I grew up in the beautiful and charming state of Connecticut. I am currently completing my Bachelors of Arts at Boston University.

My passion lies in learning and creation. I love reading and exploring what tomorrow and beyond will look like. Whether it’s technology, politics, art, or even literature, I like picking apart patterns, trends, and analyzing what’s going on. Nothing fills me with energy like finding something I can do to push the world in a new direction, which brings me to my next passion: creation. Since I was young my ambition was to build new and big. This focus has been carried over into my adulthood, although I’ve unfortunately had some difficulties on following through on it. Today as the world is in a state of revolution in virtually every field due in part to the power of computing, I am hungry to apply the latest technology on a large scale and make an impact for generic people.

This website was created in April of 2023 to help myself and others. It helps the former by providing me with a place to organize my works and give them more structure. It helps the latter by providing a great glimpse into my own mind, thoughts, and habits. If there’s anything on this site that catches your attention please reach out!
