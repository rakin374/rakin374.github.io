Hello from myself



To descibe a human, in a few paragraphs or less, is a difficult and errorprone task. Every human is involved in many parts of the the worldly affairs and exists as a mixture of archetypes. One human can take on the being of siblings, fathers, nurses, geniuses, and more. Sometimes simultaneously. In each of these stories/dramas, they are their own character. They may be heros in some, villians in others. They may be moral in one and a morality criminal in the other. Describing everything a person is into a short essay is probably the greatest data reduction problem. There is however good news in this. We humans can start new dramas, impact old ones, and engage in the choices of our life. This is my philosophy to human life.
