About Me - in progress

To descibe a human, in a few paragraphs or less, is a difficult and errorprone task. Every human is involved in many parts of the the worldly affairs and exists as a superposition of archetypes. One human can take on the being of siblings, fathers, nurses, scoundrels, geniuses, and more. Sometimes simultaneously. Nevertheless there is good news in this. Though their roles and lives can be immensily broad and diffiuclt to hash, their being is unlimited. This is my philosophy to life.
